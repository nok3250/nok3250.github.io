5. Advertising and Consent (GDPR / EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), including Poland, we will ask for your consent before showing personalised ads via Google AdMob.

  • If you grant consent, AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show ads relevant to your interests.
  • If you decline consent, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised.
  • You can withdraw or change your consent at any time through the App's privacy settings or by contacting us at flattyby@gmail.com.

Google AdMob processes ad-related data under its own terms. Data may be transferred to Google servers in the United States under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

6. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14 and later, we request permission to access your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. This identifier is used solely for delivering relevant ads via Google AdMob.

  • If you allow tracking, personalised ads may be shown.
  • If you decline, non-personalised ads will be shown instead.

You can change this permission at any time in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Flatty.

7. International Data Transfers

We use services provided by Google LLC, which is based in the United States. When data is transferred outside the EEA, Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as the legal mechanism to ensure adequate data protection. For more information, see Google's data transfer FAQ.

8. Data Retention

We retain automatically collected technical data (logs, crash reports) for no longer than 12 months. Data stored locally on your device (favourites, filters, notes) remains until you delete the App or clear its data. We do not operate user accounts, so there is no account deletion process.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at flattyby@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights (EEA / GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — you can request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to restrict how we process your data.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at flattyby@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Poland, this is:
